Welcome to the home of Vallian Arabians. I am glad you have taken the time to share in my passion. I believe that God instills certain passions in all of us. I hope as you turn these pages, you will experience a glimpse of the beautiful Arabian horses that reside here and the quality they display. It has always been my dream since I was a little girl to breed Arabians, and as a breeder for over 15 years, my goal has been to achieve beauty, conformation, athleticism and trainability. I feel I am accomplishing that. These bloodlines comprise some of the best of the performance gene pool, and I only strive to get better with each years foal crop.

I would like to thank Sheila Varian, who has been my hero and my mentor, and without her ‘Varian influence’ on me and in the blood my horses carry in their veins, I would likely not be where I am today. I wish to continue to preserve the breed and be a caretaker of the Arabian for years to come.

Sincerely,
~Valri
